About GT Movies

GT Movies is a project by GT student Anshul Gokul.

Here, you can browse a carefully curated collection of films, from the latest blockbusters to timeless classics. The site is designed for simplicity and speed — making it easy to explore movies, add them to your cart, and keep track of your orders.

GT Movies is more than just a demo store — it’s a showcase of how modern web frameworks can bring creative ideas to life, while celebrating the cinematic experience.
